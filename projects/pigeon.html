Pigeon

Logo Design

The Inspiration

The pigeon logo came about from the idea of creating a new podcasting app, the idea behind this app was to allow it’s users easy access to the information they want to listen to. And so the homing pigeon was my inspiration for the logo and app name, homing pigeons travel over extremely long distances to deliver their messages. And so it made perfect sense to adopt the humble homing pigeon as the logo for the podcasting app.

The Process

The process started with a pen on paper sketch, where the decision to create the logo based on circles was made. It was also decided that the pigeon should have a playful and friendly look as if it is always happy to deliver it’s message.

The process continued on illustrator where the logo got it’s proper shape and it’s color variation. The shapes followed the pen and paper sketch with a few alterations, and the colours where as well inspired by our favourite common pigeon.
