Designing the Absolute Zero VR 360º Video Camera App

The Goal

Designing a mobile app that controlled a complicated product meant that the main design goal for the app was simplicity, the app needed to make it easy for any user to create amazing 360º video and photo content. This made the user’s our center of attention and the primarily influencers of the app features and overall design.

The Process

The process started with a user research to understand what where the most important features the app needed, after the features where defined the work consisted on designing the wireframes which where also tested with the users and after making the appropriate adjustments, coming up with the high quality mockups.
